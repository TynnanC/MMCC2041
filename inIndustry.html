How can The Internet of Things impact industries?

Industrial Internet of Things is considered the fourth industrial revolution. Cutting edge technology and Internet of Things platforms enable increased efficiency in manufacturing industries. Machines can be monitored for their performances to ensure they are not wasting resources or energy. Near-real-time analytics enable decision making to suit day-to-day changes in operations or the environment. The use of artificial intelligence can enable machines to detect defects during production or replace human labour in repetitive tasks such as ensuring labels are positioned where needed.

Machine to Machine (M2M) communication and using the cloud has enabled wireless automation and control of machines. This reduces the cost of human resources, and in events such as Covid-19 or during malfunctions, allow operation to occur while social distancing and staying away from dangerous situations.
